Tuna Pasta

A delicious treat

Taste the absolutely delicious and unforgettable pasta,

after you've had it once you can never go back!!

Ingredients

  • 240 g of penne rigate
  • 2 cans of Tuna (160g)
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 spoon of parsley
  • a bit of olive oil
  • a bit of salt

Procedure

  1. To prepare the pasta with white tuna, first boil the water to cook the pasta.

    Drain the tuna from the preserving oil and transfer it to a bowl, shredding it with a fork.

    Wash and dry the lemon and grate the zest quite finely, letting it fall back into the bowl.

    Squeeze the juice and set it aside.

  2. Prepare an emulsion with the lemon juice and 4 tablespoons of oil and add it to the tuna.

    Finely chop the mint with the parsley and add them. Mix.

  3. In the meantime, you will have boiled the pennette al dente: drain them, keeping a few spoonfuls

    of the cooking water, and transfer them to the soup bowl. Mix well and add a little cooking water if necessary.

    Divide the pasta with white tuna into individual plates, garnish with a few mint leaves, then serve.
