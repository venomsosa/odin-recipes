Chocolates with fresh fruit

A sweet adventure

To end a dinner on a sweet note , there is nothing better than chocolates with fresh fruit.

It will be a nice way to accompany your guests towards the final chat and coffee.

These chocolates are delicious, easy to make, cold as an ice lolly and inviting as a sweet treat.

Ingredients

For 15 chocolates

  • 300 g of dark chocolate
  • 50 g of blackberries
  • 50 g of apricots
  • 50 g of raspberries

To garnish

  • fresh mint
  • grated coconut

Procedure

  1. Chocolates with fresh fruit can be prepared in a few minutes, start by

    melting the previously chopped dark chocolate in a bain-marie.

    Meanwhile, wash the fresh fruit and cut the apricots into wedges.

  2. Take a chocolate mold and place a spoonful of melted chocolate in each

    cavity of the mold and garnish with fresh fruit as desired. Put in the freezer

    for at least 3 hours

  3. Unmold the chocolates with fresh fruit and garnish

    with fresh mint and grated coconut.
